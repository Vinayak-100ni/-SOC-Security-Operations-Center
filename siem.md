## ğŸ”¹ What does SIEM provide?

SIEM mainly provides:

1. **Log collection**

   * Takes logs from servers, firewalls, applications, databases, etc.

2. **Monitoring dashboard**

   * You can see what is happening in your system in real time

3. **Threat detection**

   * Detects unusual or suspicious activities such as:

     * Too many failed logins
     * Access from unknown country
     * Malware activity

4. **Alerting**

   * Sends alert when something bad or suspicious happens

5. **Reporting**

   * Creates security reports for audits and management

6. **Investigation (forensics)**

   * Helps security analysts check:

     * Who hacked?
     * From where?
     * At what time?
     * What data was touched?
